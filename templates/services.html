Want to keep your property looking great all year? We’ve got you covered. Whether you’ve got a cozy backyard or a sprawling estate, we offer:

  • Lawn Mowing & Edging: We’ll keep your lawn looking neat and tidy with regular mowing and crisp edges.
  • Garden Maintenance: Pruning, weeding, and making sure your plants, shrubs, and trees are happy and healthy.
  • Hedge Trimming: We’ll shape and trim your hedges so they look neat and tidy.
  • Mulching & Soil Health: Fresh mulch and soil treatments to keep your garden looking fresh and vibrant.
  • Seasonal Cleanups: Clearing out leaves, debris, and getting your garden ready for the next season.

We know that acreage properties have their own unique challenges, and we’re equipped to handle larger spaces with care and attention.

Keeping your commercial or strata property looking top-notch is what we do best. Here’s how we can help:

  • Routine Groundskeeping: Regular mowing, trimming, weed control, and garden care to keep everything looking its best.
  • Tree & Shrub Maintenance: Pruning and trimming to keep your trees and shrubs in great shape.
  • Waste Management: We’ll take care of green waste, litter, and debris so your property stays clean and tidy.

We work closely with property managers and strata committees to make sure our services fit your needs perfectly.

Getting ready to sell or just want to give your property a fresh look? We’re here to help you make the best first impression:

  • Garden Makeovers: We’ll breathe new life into tired gardens with new plants, fresh mulch, and a polished finish.
  • Exterior Cleanups: We’ll handle the pressure washing, leaf removal, and general tidying up.
  • Planting & Mulching: Adding seasonal flowers, shrubs and mulch to make your property pop.
